4. The Antikythera Community: The Social Laboratory [#](#4-the-antikythera-community-the-social-laboratory)


While we build our digital sovereignty on the blockchain, we have been running a parallel experiment at [dominio.deft.work](https://dominio.deft.work): The Antikythera Community.


This project started as a simple reservation system for .IA domains, but it has evolved into something much more ambitious: a gamified Social Marketplace that serves as a functional prototype of our future digital nation.


4.1. The Economy of Drachmas [#](#4-1-the-economy-of-drachmas)


We have implemented a complete economic system based on Drachmas, our internal digital currency:



* Hierarchical Rank System: From "Polites" (citizen) to "Archon" (elite), each citizen progresses through active participation


* Daily Rewards: Incentives to build the habit of participation


* Economy of Tasks: Users can create and complete gamified missions, earning Drachmas for contributing to the project


* Domain Marketplace: Buy, sell, and exchange .IA domains with full traceability of transactions



4.2. Implemented Social Features [#](#4-2-implemented-social-features)


The platform has grown to become a true community space:



* Community Forum: Discussions on domains, strategy, and project vision


* Follower System: Building social networks within the community


* Push Notifications: Real-time communication with citizens


* User Profiles: Each citizen has their digital identity with an avatar, alias, and statistics


* Domain Files: Each reserved domain requires a justification of its purpose and a representative image



4.3. Technical Infrastructure [#](#4-3-technical-infrastructure)


The project is built on modern and scalable technology:



* Stack: React + Vite + Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Storage, Cloud Functions)


* Dockerized: Prepared for distributed deployment


* CORS Configured: Secure upload of images and assets


* Analytics Integrated: Tracking metrics and user behavior


* Responsive Design: Optimized for mobile and desktop



4.4. The Vision: From Experiment to Infrastructure [#](#4-4-the-vision-from-experiment-to-infrastructure)


The Antikythera Community is not just a side project. It is the testing ground where we are validating the governance, economic, and participation mechanisms that we will eventually migrate to our Web3 infrastructure.


Each feature implemented here is a social experiment. Each Drachma earned is a data point on incentives. Each domain reserved is a vote of confidence in the future we are building.


Now that .anticitera is officially ours, this community will be the seed of our first generation of digital citizens.
